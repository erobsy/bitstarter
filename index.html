Would you like to get some help from time to time? Do you enjoy helping people? Do you want to get help for free and also help other people sharing your talents and habilities? If so, you are really lucky, because that is the exact philosophy of Favoralia, the social network for exchanging free favours.
Sounds interesting? Of course it does! And it is even better because it already exists and it has been kicking for more than one year now: more than 6,000 people from almost 50 countries are actively using this platform. At the moment, more than 4,000 favours are being offered in Favoralia.

So, if it is already up and running, why should you contribute? Because Favoralia was designed just to be used in our home country, Spain, and thus was fully written in Spanish. Several months later, we launched it in Latin America. But now we want to make it global: we need to translate the whole site to English, launch the international version, introduce a responsive framework, improve the design and usability and upgrade the server to cope with higher traffic and functionality. Do you want to help us? If so, contribute with any amount of money in any currency available. Our goal is helping people and improving a bit our little planet: would you join us?

FAQ

What means "free" in "free favour"?
Means free as in "free beer": no money involved. Favoralia is a free platform: you do not have to pay anything to get/give from/to others. That is why we need some funding right now!
Is Favoralia a time bank?
In a way, it is very similar to a time bank, but you do not trade time for time as it is. You offer a favour an set up a number of points, and you will get them from other users as you do it. We prefer this system because it provides a more efficient "favour market".
So, in a nutshell, what is Favoralia?
Well, the best definition we can think of is Fiverr without money. Nothing more. Nothing less. Do it for the sake of good karma and get some points to have new favours done to you.
